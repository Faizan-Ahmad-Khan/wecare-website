WeCare's Work

WeCare works in over 190 countries and territories to save children's lives, to defend their rights, and to help them fulfil their potential, from early childhood through adolescence. And we never give up.

Discover WeCare's work for every child, everywhere.

Supporting equity, diversity and inclusion programming

Equity, diversity and inclusion have always been key pillars of our WeCare Schools and WeCare Teachers programming. We continue to be deeply committed to supporting youth and educators with resources to help further understanding and to take meaningful action on these important topics.
